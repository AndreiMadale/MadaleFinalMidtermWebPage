The Philippines Best Pickup Truck for its Price 2019

Foton Thunder 4x4

Previous Next

Power is one of the most important aspects that experts are critiquing pick-up trucks for these days. FOTON was clearly paying attention when they created and unleashed their own turbo-diesel pickup – the Thunder. It runs on a 2.8L 161 hp turbo charged diesel engine attached to a 5-speed manual tranny capable of 360 Nm of torque. The Thunder also provides exceptional value for money and as a result may be a more practical and cost effective choice for local Philippine businessmen or adventurer. The Thunder also has a slew of safety measures in the form of seatbelt reminder, child safety lock, dual airbags, and reverse sensor. Other features include interior lights off delay, remote control power windows, and leather seats and because of the Philippines' unforgiving environment from bumps, cracks and potholes on the road it wont be an issue hence earning its spot as the best pickup for its price of 2019.
