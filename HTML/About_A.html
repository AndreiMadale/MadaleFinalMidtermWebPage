Car Enthusiasm as a Hobby and it's Pros and Cons


Loving Cars Can Be An Expensive Hobby, Let’s be honest with ourselves,
being a gearhead can be a serious drain to your personal bank account.
Sure, you can buy and modify cars on a budget but it’s easy to get
carried away with modifications and options. Also, it’s a frustrating
reality that some of the most desirable cars can also cost the
most money to fill up with gas, maintain, buy and insure.


Being A Petrolhead Can Be Dangerous To Yourself And Others Many famous
people have met their demise because of a tragic car accident such as Aryton
Senna, James Dean, Paul Walker and Dale Earnhardt just to name a few.
Sometimes we forget when driving fast how close
we can be to making a fatal mistake.


Gearheads Don’t Often Have The Best Relationship With Law Enforcement
and The General Public A lot of what car guys do is frowned upon by both
the general public and the police. Loud exhausts, rolling coal, street
racing, speeding, burnouts and drifting are things that can make our
neighbors hate us and the cops give us hefty fines or even prison sentences. .

What Does it Mean to be a Car Enthusiast?

Brian Alexander Tan
Information Technology Student of USTP CDO

A car enthusiast is someone who loves cars and everything about them; enjoys buying them, collecting them,
working on them, modifying them and anything else that comes to mind.
We love performance,style, handling, horsepower, speed,
even the smell and sound of them.

Matthew Erwin Batolina
IT Student of XU CDO

Being a car enthusiast means sharing a certain love for something that not everyone would normally likely relate to.
There’s just something very special about the suave curvatures and dips in the design of what makes a car
what it is. Not to mention, the way it just sings when purring out it’s regal exhaust notes.
But most importantly, a car enthusiast is all in it for the thrill. The thrill of
flying through speed. Speed is at the heart of every car enthusiast, and
that’s all that matters.
